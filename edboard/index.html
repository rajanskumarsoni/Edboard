About Us

The Editorial Board is the official literary sub-council and one of the earliest established boards of the university. As the name suggests, it primarily focuses on conducting literary events and strives throughout the year to conduct events that not only foster grooming of one's skills at the literary level but also provide a platform for the holistic development of the students. The main events conducted by the board include Malaviyan Ace, Arunoday, SpellCzar and Udgaar. The board also publishes literary pieces such as the annual university magazine 'Malvika' and the monthly newsletter 'Tiresia'. Along with these, the board coordinates other activities like debates and discussions round the year. Like the other prevailing boards, The Editorial Board forms a vital part of the curriculum vitae of the university and wishes to continue the same in the years to come with the flare to work ferociously in the right direction.
