UDGAAR 2017
About
Conducted towards the end of the even semester, Udgaar is one of the most exciting events organised by the Editorial board. With its vibrant and festive vibe, the event promises a break from the mundane routines, thereby revitalising the spirit of the participants. The event is an amalgamation of several different competitions which are exotic and commoving. The main contests that are conducted during the three-day event include House of commons (A mock session of the parliament held as a debate competition concluding with the rejection or passing of a bill), pot decoration, sketching, Don de Paperika (the students walk the ramp clad in dresses made out of paper), Rangoli competition, Kite Runner and many more. From the test of wits in House of Commons to Nukkad Naatak where performers run the whole gamut of human emotions, This year Udgaar comes with a promise to leave everyone spellbound. These events are not only unique but also promote the idea of having fun and learning instead of winning and losing. Udgaar 2017 will be a festival of colours, mirth and bonds that gives the Malaviyans several memories to take back home.
